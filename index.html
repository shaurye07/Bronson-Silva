about

Bronson Silva Cycles is an old school bike shop focused on customer service. The folks that work here and visit the shop are people who love to ride.

about us

Owner and frame builder

Bronson Silva is a certified bike mechanic turned frame builder. He’s owned this bike shop for over 5 years and has been working/building bikes and unicycles for over 15 years.

He’s ridden most of California on a touring bike and toured the alps on a unicycle. His eye for detail, passion for perfection, and years of riding are the quintessential combination for frame building. His frame builds always start with a conversation to ensure the end product will satisfy not only your eye but your style as well.

Master Mechanic

Tahn Rehmer is a certified master bicycle mechanic and a renowned wheel builder. He’s been wrenching on bicycles for 20 years and has hand-built over 10,000 wheels in his career.

Cyclocross, track, cross country, downhill, road, triathletes, fixies, and single speeds are some of his disciplines.In addition to building wheels he is certified to maintain/rebuild that tricky front/rear suspension. There really isn’t much he can’t fix on a bike and he makes a great cup of coffee.

service

The shop has a full service and custom fabrication area to fix/build just about anything.It doesn’t matter if you race, cruise, or climb the mountains like a goat, this shop has the experience to satisfy your biking needs.

WHEEL BUILDING

We build all types of wheels to fit your bike, 700c, 26 inch, 650B. We usually stock DT-Swiss, Mavic, Velo-Orange rims as well as White Industries, Phil Wood, and Shimano hubs

BIKE FITTING

We use the "Fit Kit" to help customize your existing/new frame to fit your body type. Common changes include but are not limited to stem length/angle, seat height/angle/rotation, and handlebar style/height.

CUSTOMIZATION

Silva Cycles specializes in hand built frames to match your riding style with dream bicycle frame. All frames are fit to your body proportions to maximize power, comfort and efficiency.

MAINTENANCE

Our mechanics have over 35 years of experience wrenching on bikes. We specialize in modern internal hub gearing, generator lighting, mountain bike suspensions, and modifications for greater comfort and utility.

Our Services

Silva Cycles specializes in hand built frames to meet the needs of any avid cyclist. Custom frames allow an infinite number of options for both function and looks.
